I'm Risto, a software developer based in Macedonia with around a decade of experience building backend systems - from high-throughput media pipelines to distributed control planes.

I currently work at **Ludo Technology** as a Software Developer and Tech Lead, where I've shipped production systems across monitoring, VoIP, A/B testing, and Google Ads tooling. Most of my work there lives in Go and TypeScript, running on Kubernetes and GCP. On the deeper end, I've built control plane services that manage service placement via DHTs, handle mTLS between services acting as an internal CA, and implement distributed consensus with Raft.

Before that I spent four years at **Miracle Mill** working on a large-scale video streaming platform and a high-performance ordering system for warehouses and restaurant chains — mostly on AWS, with heavy use of Lambda, DynamoDB, SNS/SQS, and CloudFront.

Observability has been a constant thread across all of it: structured logging, metrics, distributed tracing, Prometheus, Honeycomb, CloudWatch. I care about systems that are understandable in production, not just at deploy time.
